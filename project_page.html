Introduction

      Role: UI Designer, Researcher
  • Tools: Pen & paper, Adobe XD
  • Objective: Design a responsive web App designed for the woman who wants to look and feel good with minimal effort at home.

    • Background

    This is a project I completed as a part of a CareerFoundry UI Design course.

    Problem

    Many women don't wear the same clothing at home that would be worn outside. Because women are working remotely now than ever, there is a need for lounge style clothing that allows for comfort and style where woman can look good with minimum effort . Our online store offers a variety of products that beautify you and your surroundings.

    Solution

    I thought I would design a clothing line for women 30 & up can level up her style with minimal effort and still feel she dressed herself for the day
